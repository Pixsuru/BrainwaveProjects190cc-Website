About Us

Welcome to Brainwave Projects 190cc, your premier destination for Interior Decorating, Manufacturing, and Installation services, proudly holding a BEE Level 1 accreditation.


Our dedicated team of seasoned consultants and skilled professionals is committed to delivering unparalleled excellence in both the domestic and corporate hospitality sectors.


At Brainwave Projects, we seamlessly navigate projects from their conceptualization stage through meticulous design, precise manufacturing, and finally, the flawless installation of all furniture, fittings, and related equipment. With over 15 years of industry expertise, our family-owned business combines a distinctive touch with unwavering professionalism to bring your vision to life. Experience the difference with Brainwave Projects 190cc.

About Our Company

Brainwave Projects 190cc is the brainchild of Jerome and Mathilda January. Established in June 2001, our journey began in the picturesque southern suburbs of Cape Town.
Since then, we’ve been dedicated to crafting exceptional interiors and delivering impeccable service to our valued clients.

Brainwave Projects employs a team of seasoned professionals to deliver unparalleled FF & E installation and disposition service to hotel owners and developers. Regardless of how large or small the project is our team can skilfully manage the job. Whether you’re renovating ten rooms of an intimate bed and breakfast or 2000 guestrooms and public spaces within your large resort.

Brainwave Projects has the experience, skills, and tools to make certain your projects are executed flawlessly, from concept to completion.
